Treasurehunt

Yarrrrh, time to dig up some treasure! Scan the QR-code or go to byjagt.septima.dk to begin finding the secret digital treasures hidden all over Copenhagen. This progressive web app was developed with Septima P/S as eye candy at their booth at the Kortdage conference.

In addition to guiding the user to some of the hidden gems all over Copenhagen, this is also a multiplayer experience. The movement of all other pirates in the area and notifications of their progress is shown in real time.
